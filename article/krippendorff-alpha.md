# When Agreement Is an Illusion

**Subtitle:** Statistical foundations of inter-annotator agreement — from observed agreement to Krippendorff's Alpha

> **Draft scaffold.** This file is the long-form article source. Sections will be written in **Phase 5** following `docs/outline.md`. Theory and figures come from `notes/` and `figures/` as each phase completes.

## Working thesis

> An agreement score of 0.80 among annotators is more likely evidence of structured noise than of true consensus when chance agreement is not properly accounted for. Krippendorff's Alpha models disagreement under randomness and generalises across raters, missing data, and measurement scales.
