**Status:** scaffold — derivations to be added in Phase 1 (roadmap issues #5–#7).

- Definition of observed agreement \(A_o\) from pairwise agreeing decisions.

- Worked examples for \(K=2,3\) and imbalanced \(\pi\).
- Core insight: high \(A_o\) does **not** imply high reliability.
